About Matchagana

Matchagana started life as a hiragana to romaji matching game. Initially developed in 2020, Matchagana aims to provide a fun way to learn to memorise hiragana characters to their romaji counterparts.

There are future updates planned, including a Matchagana Katakana game mode, leaderboards and timers.
