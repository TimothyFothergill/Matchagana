About Matchagana

Matchagana started life as a hiragana to romaji matching game. Initially developed in 2020, Matchagana aims to provide a fun way to learn to memorise hiragana characters to their romaji counterparts.

There are future updates planned, including leaderboards and timers.

I decided to develop this in my spare time as a way to cement my Japanese learning and also learning software development principles.
